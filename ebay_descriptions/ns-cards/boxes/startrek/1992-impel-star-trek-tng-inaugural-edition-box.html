DESCRIPTION

This is a factory sealed wax box from the 1992 Impel Star Trek: The Next Generation Inaugural Edition trading card series — the definitive first edition release for TNG collectors. Each box contains 36 sealed packs for a total of 360 cards, featuring beloved characters including Captain Jean-Luc Picard and Deanna Troi from the iconic TV series.

The artwork throughout this set is credited to Frank Frazetta, lending the cards a dramatic painted quality that set this release apart from standard photo card offerings of the era. As a 1st Edition release manufactured by Impel in the United States, this box represents an important milestone in non-sport Star Trek collecting.

Sealed first edition wax boxes from this early-90s series are increasingly difficult to source, making this a compelling addition for TNG fans and vintage non-sport collectors alike.
