DESCRIPTION

This is a factory-sealed hobby box of 1997 Inkworks Men in Black trading cards, produced as the official tie-in for the original 1997 Sony Pictures film starring Will Smith, Tommy Lee Jones, Vincent D'Onofrio, Linda Fiorentino, and Rip Torn. Each box contains 36 wax packs with 8 cards per pack, for a total of 288 cards per box.

What you get — one factory-sealed box containing:
  • 36 sealed wax packs (8 cards per pack, 288 cards total)
  • Shot at the complete 90-card base set covering scenes from the film, MiB Dossiers (Kay, Jay, Elle, Zed, Dee, HQ, Immigration, Arsenal, Toy Shop), and Aliens (Samarians, Lysians, The Squats, Vyrmidians, The Homarid, The Pug, Erraqnians, Bugs)
  • Alien Profiles Die-Cut chase cards (P1-P5) at 1:11 packs — Mikey, Yin and Yang, Skulk Brothers, Rosenberg, Edgar
  • Foilworks chase cards (S1-S5) at 1:24 packs — Alien Technology, Cover-Ups, Neuralyzer, Aliens, Men in Black Mythos
  • Cards in Black chase (B1-B2) at 1:54 packs — Jay and Kay
  • Chance at the Lowell Cunningham autograph card — signed by the creator of the original Men in Black comic series

A standout late-90s movie tie-in set with strong long-term collector demand. The Cards in Black chase and the Cunningham autograph are the headline hits.
CONDITION / GRADE

The box is factory sealed with the original shrink wrap fully intact. The exterior of the box is clean with only minor handling marks consistent with careful long-term storage. This item has not been professionally graded. Please review all photos carefully before purchasing — what you see is what you get.

Notes for collectors: The 1997 Inkworks print run is documented to include an early variation on card #87 (Pug's image absent on some early prints), and many cards in the #81-90 range can show vertical rippling that affects NM vs Mint grading. These traits are inherent to the original print run and not specific to this box.
