DESCRIPTION

This is a factory-sealed Premiere Edition hobby box of 1996 SkyBox Batman Master Series — a premium painted-art tribute to the Dark Knight featuring covers and original art from Scott Hampton, Carl Critchlow, Dermot Power, and Duncan Fegredo. The box is factory numbered 04,955 of 36,000, certifying its place in a limited production run that's far smaller than typical mid-90s SkyBox sets.

What you get — one factory-sealed box containing:
  • 24 sealed packs — 10 cards per pack
  • Shot at the complete 90-card base set (#1–90, with #90 the checklist)
  • Artist's Proof parallel — blue-back parallel cards inserted at 1 per pack (full proof set takes ~3.75 boxes)
  • Master Villains chase set — 10 cards at 1:10 packs
  • Batman Fantasy Spectra-Etch chase set — 6 cards at 1:6 packs
  • Chromium chase — 2 cards (Batman, Joker) at 1:24 packs (about 1 per box)
  • Clearchrome rare chase — 2 cards (Batman, Joker) at 1:120 packs (roughly 1 per 5 boxes)
  • Approximately 2–3 complete base sets per box if collation is perfect

Master Series is widely regarded as one of the strongest comic-art trading card sets ever produced — the painted artwork and the very low 36,000-box print run make sealed examples increasingly hard to find.
