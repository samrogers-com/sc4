DESCRIPTION

This is a factory sealed wax box from the 1984 Fleer Dune non-sport trading card series, produced to coincide with David Lynch's epic sci-fi film adaptation of Frank Herbert's legendary novel. Manufactured by Fleer in the United States, each box contains 36 sealed packs of standard-sized English-language cards.

The 1984 Dune film featured a star-studded ensemble cast including Kyle MacLachlan (Paul Atreides, his film debut), Sting (Feyd-Rautha), Patrick Stewart (Gurney Halleck), Sean Young (Chani), Max von Sydow (Doctor Kynes), Virginia Madsen (Princess Irulan), and Kenneth McMillan (Baron Vladimir Harkonnen), among many others. Cards from this set capture the film's grand visual scope and iconic characters.

Sealed wax boxes from this short-lived release are exceptionally hard to find after more than 40 years, making this a prized addition for any serious non-sport or sci-fi collector.
