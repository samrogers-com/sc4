DESCRIPTION

This is a factory sealed box from the 1991 Impel Star Trek 25th Anniversary Series 2 trading card set. Released to celebrate a quarter century of the Star Trek franchise, this set bridges both Star Trek: The Original Series and Star Trek: The Next Generation, featuring iconic actors including William Shatner and Patrick Stewart.

The box contains a total of 432 standard-sized cards and includes hologram cards as special inserts. Manufactured by Impel in the United States, this was one of the defining non-sport card releases of the early 1990s and remains a sought-after item among Star Trek collectors.

Sealed boxes from this 25th Anniversary release are increasingly difficult to find over 30 years later — a great opportunity for fans and collectors alike.
