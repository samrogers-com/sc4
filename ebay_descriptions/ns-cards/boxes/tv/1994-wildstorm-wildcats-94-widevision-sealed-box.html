DESCRIPTION

This is a factory-sealed hobby box of 1994 WildStorm WildC.A.T.s '94 Widevision trading cards — the official trading card release for Jim Lee's WildC.A.T.s, one of the flagship titles of the WildStorm imprint and the early-90s Image Comics movement. Each box contains 24 wax packs with 6 cards per pack, for a total of 144 cards per box.

What you get — one factory-sealed box containing:
  • 24 sealed wax packs in oversized Widevision format (6 cards per pack, 144 cards total)
  • Shot at the complete 96-card base set — 48 character cards, 12 variant cards, 12 swimsuit cards, 12 battle cards, 5 team cards, 1 checklist
  • Painted chase cards (12 cards) at 1:8 packs
  • Double-Sided chase cards (6 cards) at 1:12 packs — featuring Jim Lee and J. Scott Campbell artwork on opposite sides; the six cards combine into one large picture
  • WildD.I.S.C.s chase (5 cards)
  • Autograph cards (16 different) at 1:375 packs — the headline rare hit, with potential signatures from Jim Lee and other key WildStorm artists

A standout set from the peak of the Image Comics era. Collector demand for Jim Lee artwork and original WildC.A.T.s material has stayed strong, and Widevision-format chromium boxes from this period are increasingly hard to find sealed.
