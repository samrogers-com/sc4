Released in 1996 by Topps, this is the Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire Premium Trading Card Set — a complete 100-card collection tied to the landmark multimedia event that bridged The Empire Strikes Back and Return of the Jedi. The set features iconic characters including Prince Xizor, Luke Skywalker, and Darth Vader, rendered in the distinctive painted style of celebrated artists Greg and Tim Hildebrandt.

Premium card stock gives each card a substantial, high-quality feel that sets this set apart from standard base releases. A must-have for fans of the Shadows of the Empire era and Hildebrandt art collectors alike.
