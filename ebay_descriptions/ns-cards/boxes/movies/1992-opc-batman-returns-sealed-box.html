DESCRIPTION

This is a factory-sealed hobby box of 1992 O-Pee-Chee Batman Returns trading cards — the official tie-in for Tim Burton's blockbuster sequel starring Michael Keaton (Batman/Bruce Wayne), Michelle Pfeiffer (Catwoman/Selina Kyle), Danny DeVito (The Penguin/Oswald Cobblepot), and Christopher Walken (Max Shreck). Each box contains 36 wax packs.

What you get — one factory-sealed box containing:
  • 36 sealed wax packs — 5 base cards plus 1 Stadium Club insert per pack
  • Shot at the complete 89-card base set (the O-Pee-Chee version uniquely includes a checklist card #89 not present in U.S. Topps)
  • Stadium Club insert subset — 10 lettered cards (A-J) at 1 per pack, including "Batman Blows It," "Director Tim Burton enjoys...," and "Introducing the Batmissile!"
  • Approximately 2 complete base sets per box if collation is perfect

O-Pee-Chee vs. U.S. Topps — how to tell them apart:
  • O-Pee-Chee (this box): light blue and pink card backs, matte finish, Stadium Club inserts have a black border around the front image, marked "printed in Canada"
  • U.S. Topps: dark blue and red card backs, glossy finish, full-bleed Stadium Club inserts
The Canadian print run was smaller and these OPC boxes have become the harder of the two to find sealed in the U.S. market.
