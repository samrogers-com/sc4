DESCRIPTION

Following up on the success of the 1991 Impel set, SkyBox Disney Collector Cards Series II (1992) delivers 200 cards of pure Disney nostalgia. SkyBox took over the license and expanded the scope, covering not only classic animated shorts but also Disney comic strips, vintage promotional posters, and even step-by-step character drawing guides.

The Favorite Stories section returns, breaking down classic Mickey Mouse and Donald Duck cartoons into sequential moments designed to flow beautifully in nine-pocket pages. Beyond the cartoons, the checklist ventures into Disney's rich publishing history — classic comic strip panels and iconic poster art that longtime Disney fans will immediately recognize.

Three double-sided hologram insert cards were produced for this set — Get Goofy, Mickey and the Beanstalk, and Plane Crazy — randomly seeded into packs and highly collectible.
