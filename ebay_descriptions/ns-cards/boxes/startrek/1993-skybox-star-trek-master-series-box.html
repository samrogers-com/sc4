DESCRIPTION

This is a factory sealed box from the 1993 SkyBox Star Trek Master Series Edition trading card set. The Master Series is widely regarded as one of the most visually striking Star Trek card releases ever produced, featuring premium painted artwork of iconic characters and scenes from across the franchise.

The complete base set consists of 90 cards, and this sealed box gives collectors the chance to pull from the full range of beautifully illustrated cards. The set features beloved characters from the Star Trek universe, including Leonard Nimoy as Spock, among many others.

Manufactured by SkyBox in the United States, sealed boxes from this early-90s release are becoming increasingly scarce — a solid pickup for any serious Star Trek or non-sport card collector.
