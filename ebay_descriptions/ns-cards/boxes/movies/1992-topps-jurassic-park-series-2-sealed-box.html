DESCRIPTION

This is a factory-sealed hobby box of 1992 Topps Jurassic Park Series 2 trading cards — the second wave of the Topps movie tie-in, which is notably scarcer than Series 1. Each box contains 36 wax packs.

What you get — one factory-sealed box containing:
  • 36 sealed wax packs — 8 base cards plus 1 puzzle sticker per pack
  • Shot at the complete 66-card base set (cards #89–154) picking up where Series 1 left off — The Dinosaurs (#90–121), Dinosaur Art (#122–125), and Behind The Scenes (#126–154)
  • 11-card puzzle sticker set — dinosaur and park imagery, 1 per pack
  • Action Holograms chase set — four silver-finish hologram cards (Baby Velociraptor hatching, Spitter, Tyrannosaurus Rex, Velociraptor) inserted at 1 in 18 packs (about 2 per box on average)
  • Approximately 3+ complete base sets and 3+ complete sticker sets per box if collation is perfect

Series 2 is the harder of the two Topps Jurassic Park sets to find sealed — print runs were smaller and the holograms in particular have held collector value over time.
