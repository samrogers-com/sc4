DESCRIPTION

This is a factory sealed wax box from the 1996 Topps Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire Premium Trading Card series. Each box contains 36 sealed packs of standard-sized non-sport trading cards featuring iconic characters including Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, and Prince Xizor from the Shadows of the Empire multimedia storyline.

The artwork throughout this set was created by acclaimed painters Greg and Tim Hildebrandt, whose richly illustrated style made this one of the standout non-sport releases of its era. Manufactured by Topps in the United States, this is a true piece of mid-90s Star Wars collecting history.

Sealed boxes from this set are increasingly hard to find and represent a great opportunity for Star Wars collectors and non-sport card enthusiasts alike.
