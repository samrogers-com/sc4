About Sam's Collectibles

I started in 1983 when I purchased my first Star Wars movie poster and got hooked on collecting. Over time, I accumulated around 70 posters. Unfortunately, due to damage from moving, only about 30 posters remain, and they are currently available for sale.

Following my love for Star Wars, I began collecting Star Wars Marvel comic books from 1977 and later moved on to Star Wars Dark Horse comics. This collection continues to be a cherished part of my memorabilia.

I then got hooked on non-sports trading cards, with Star Wars and Star Trek making up the bulk of my collection. Today, non-sports trading cards are the cornerstone of my collectibles, with a focus on the worlds and universes that captivated me.

Almost all of my posters were purchased between 1983 and 1990 from reputable poster dealers — well before the bootleg era — and my card collection spans hundreds of sealed boxes and complete sets from that golden era of collecting.
