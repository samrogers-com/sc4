DESCRIPTION

This is a factory sealed box from the 1993 SkyBox Star Trek: Deep Space Nine Series 1 — Inaugural Edition. As the very first trading card release for DS9, this set holds a special place in Star Trek collecting history. Each box contains 36 sealed packs with 8 cards per pack for a total of 288 cards.

The base set consists of 100 cards with subsets featuring characters, technology, first-season episodes, foreign languages, and trivia — all sourced from original digital master imagery. A special Technical Card subset showcases schematics and technical details from the station and its fleet.

Beyond the base set, boxes may contain some of the most sought-after inserts from this release:
  • SPECTRA Cards — Four unique foil-enhanced cards, randomly packed (~1:18 packs)
  • SPECTRA GOLD Card — One limited edition gold foil card, randomly inserted (~1:72 packs)
  • Redemption Cards — Redeemable for an exclusive 10-card foil-stamped BONUS set not available in stores (~1:180 packs)
Manufactured by SkyBox in the United States, sealed Inaugural Edition boxes from this early DS9 release are becoming increasingly rare — a must-have for any serious Star Trek or non-sport card collector.
