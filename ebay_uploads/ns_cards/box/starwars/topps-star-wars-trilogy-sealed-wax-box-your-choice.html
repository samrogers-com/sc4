This listing offers your choice of sealed wax boxes from the original Topps Star Wars trilogy card sets — one of the most celebrated non-sport card runs in collecting history. All boxes are factory sealed. Select your preferred series from the variation menu.

The set that started it all. Five series were released to keep up with the film's phenomenal demand. Each sealed box contains 36 packs, with 7 cards + 1 sticker per pack.

Documenting the darkest chapter of the trilogy. Each sealed box contains 36 packs, with 12 cards + 1 sticker per pack.

The epic conclusion. Each sealed box contains 36 packs, with 10 cards + 1 sticker per pack. Stickers feature 6 color variations: Purple, Turquoise, Green, Yellow, Red, and Orange.
• Approx. 2.73 common sets per box • Approx. 1.09 sticker sets per box
(Assumes perfect collation — actual results will vary)
