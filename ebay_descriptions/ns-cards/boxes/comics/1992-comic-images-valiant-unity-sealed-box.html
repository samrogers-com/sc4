DESCRIPTION

This is a factory-sealed hobby box of 1992 Comic Images Valiant Unity: Time Is Not Absolute — the official trading card chronicle of Valiant Comics' first universe-wide crossover event, with art and characters drawn from Valiant's full early-90s lineup. The set is widely regarded as one of the strongest comic-publisher trading-card releases of the era. Each box contains 48 wax packs (heavier configuration than the standard 36-pack box).

What you get — one factory-sealed box containing:
  • 48 sealed packs — 10 cards per pack
  • Shot at the complete 90-card base set covering the full Unity storyline across X-O Manowar, Solar, Shadowman, Harbinger, Eternal Warrior, Archer & Armstrong, Magnus Robot Fighter, Rai, and Turok
  • Valiant Rookies Chromium chase set — 6 cards (Bloodshot, Rai, Turok, Hotshot, Rai and the Future Force, Screen) at 1:6 packs (~8 per box) — chromium technology was unusual in 1992 and these still photograph beautifully
  • Approximately 5 complete base sets per box if collation is perfect

The Unity crossover is a foundational moment in Valiant Comics history and these cards are increasingly hard to find sealed. The chromium rookie subset is the marquee chase — eight pulls per box on average means a real shot at the full 6-card chromium set in one break.
