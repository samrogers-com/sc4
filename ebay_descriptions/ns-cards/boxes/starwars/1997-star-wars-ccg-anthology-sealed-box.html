DESCRIPTION

This is a sealed box from the Star Wars Customizable Card Game: Anthology I, produced by Decipher Inc. in 1997. The Anthology series was a special edition release designed to bring together exclusive cards featuring characters and scenes from across the Star Wars universe, making it a sought-after set among CCG collectors and Star Wars enthusiasts alike.

Decipher's Star Wars CCG was the premier Star Wars card game of its era, known for its deep gameplay and stunning card artwork. Sealed product from this period is increasingly difficult to find in this condition.
