DESCRIPTION

Released in early 1992 by Impel Marketing — the same company behind the iconic Marvel Universe sets — DC Cosmic Cards: Inaugural Edition brought the DC Universe to the trading card world for the first time in a major way. The 180-card base set celebrates DC's greatest heroes and villains across three distinct comic eras: Golden Age, Silver Age, and Modern Age.

The first 30 cards trace the evolution of ten of DC's most iconic characters — including Superman, Batman, Wonder Woman, and The Flash — with three cards each charting their Golden Age origins, Silver Age reinvention, and Modern Age appearance. The rest of the checklist expands into team books, villains, supporting characters, and the broader DC cosmos.

Chase card collectors will be hunting for the Hologram Hall of Fame inserts — 10 foil hologram cards featuring DC's A-list heroes, randomly seeded throughout packs.
