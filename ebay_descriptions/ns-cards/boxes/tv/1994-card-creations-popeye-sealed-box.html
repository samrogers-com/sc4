DESCRIPTION

This is a factory-sealed hobby box of 1994 Card Creations Popeye trading cards, released to celebrate the 65th anniversary of E.C. Segar's iconic spinach-eating sailor — first appearing in the Thimble Theatre comic strip on January 17, 1929. Each box contains 36 wax packs with 6 cards per pack, for a total of 216 cards per box.

What you get — one factory-sealed box containing:
  • 36 sealed wax packs (6 cards per pack, 216 cards total)
  • Shot at the complete 100-card base set covering character profiles, comic history, cartoon history, and character situations
  • Character Foils chase cards (12 cards) at 1:9 packs
  • Evolution Chrome chase cards (8 cards) at 1:36 packs — ~1 per box on average
  • Power Cels chase cards (3 cards) at 1:360 packs — the headline rare insert (only 1929 Casetopper boxes were produced, so Power Cels are genuinely scarce)
  • Eligible for the wrapper mail-in offer when active

A standout cartoon/comic-strip themed set with strong long-term collector appeal among Popeye fans, classic-comics collectors, and 90s non-sport hobby completists. The Power Cels chase remains the white whale of the set.
