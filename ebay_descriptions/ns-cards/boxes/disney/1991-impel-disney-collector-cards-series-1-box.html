DESCRIPTION

Released in 1991 by Impel Marketing, Disney Collector Cards Series 1 is a vibrant tribute to the golden era of Disney animation. The 210-card base set is organized into three memorable sections that showcase the stories, characters, and world of classic Disney.

Favorite Stories takes collectors through 11 beloved shorts starring Mickey Mouse, Donald Duck, and pals, breaking each cartoon down into key story moments. The cards are designed to flow perfectly in nine-pocket pages. Family Portraits offers character close-ups across multiple cartoons with biographical info on the back. World Tour rounds things out with the wider Disney universe.

Two double-sided hologram insert cards were produced for this set and are randomly seeded into packs — among the most sought-after pieces for Disney card collectors.
