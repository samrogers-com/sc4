# Poster & Comic Book Restoration Guide

## Poster Linen-Backing Analysis

### What is Linen-Backing?
Professional mounting of a poster onto acid-free linen fabric. The process:
1. Poster is laid flat on linen using archival adhesive
2. Folds are flattened, tears are repaired
3. Touch-up paint can restore missing color
4. Result: flat, stable poster that can be framed

### Cost
- Standard one-sheet (27x41): **$150 - $300** per poster
- With restoration (tear repair, paint touch-up): **$250 - $500**
- Turnaround: 2-6 weeks depending on the shop

### Impact on Value

| **Damaged posters** (C-5 or below) | $200 | $400-$600 | **Worth it** (saves the poster) |

## Comic Book Restoration Analysis

### Types of Professional Restoration

| Treatment | What it does | Cost | Time | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Cleaning** | Removes surface dirt, fingerprints, light stains | $20-$50 | 1-2 hours | Books VG+ or better with surface grime |
| **Pressing** | Flattens folds, bends, spine rolls using heat/pressure | $15-$30 | 1-4 weeks | Books with bends, light creases, non-color-breaking |
| **Pressing + Cleaning combo** | Both | $30-$75 | 1-4 weeks | Most common service |
| **Page whitening** | Chemical or UV treatment to restore page color | $50-$150 | Variable | Books with tanned/brown pages where white would boost grade |
| **Tear repair** | Adhesive repair of tears | $25-$100 | Variable | Significant tears that drop grade |
| **Color touch** | Paint touch-up on color-breaking creases | $50-$200 | Variable | Key issues where grade bump = big value jump |

### Important Notes
- CBCS and CGC **require disclosure** of any restoration
- Pressing is generally NOT considered restoration (it's conservation)
- Cleaning is a gray area — light cleaning is usually accepted
- Color touch and tear repair ARE restoration and must be disclosed
- Restored books get a different label (CGC "Purple" label) which sells for less
- **Pressing + cleaning are the safest treatments** — no negative label impact
