## Provenance
All posters purchased between 1983-1990 from 1-2 reputable poster dealers. This predates the known bootleg era (mid-to-late 1980s bootlegs), which is a strong authenticity selling point for eBay listings and collectors.

## Grading Standard
All posters graded on the movie poster C1-C10 scale (see docs/movie-poster-grading-scale.md). Factory folds on pre-1980 one-sheets are NOT considered defects.

## For eBay Listings — Always Include:
- Specific style designation (Style A/B/C/D)
- Printer codes (77/21-0, 80000, R810129, etc.)
- Authentication markers (GAU logo, NSS codes)
- Provenance: "Purchased 1983-1990 from reputable poster dealer, predating the known bootleg era"
- Grade and condition description with specific defects noted
- For ESB Advance: mention the Billy Dee Williams recall making originals rarer
- For Style D: note edge damage specifically
- For Signed Struzan: mention edition number and artist signature
