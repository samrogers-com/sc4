This is a sealed hobby box of 1993 Topps Batman: The Animated Series 1 trading cards — the first wave of Topps' tie-in for the Bruce Timm / Paul Dini animated series that aired on Fox Kids from 1992-1995 and redefined Batman for an entire generation. Each box contains 36 wax packs with 10 cards per pack, for a total of 360 cards per box.
