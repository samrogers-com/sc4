DESCRIPTION

This is a sealed box of 1993 SkyBox X-Men Series 2 trading cards — the second wave of SkyBox's massively popular X-Men trading card series, released alongside the launch of the X-Men animated TV series. Each box contains 36 wax packs with 6 cards per pack, for a total of 216 cards per box.

What you get — one sealed box containing:
  • 36 sealed wax packs (6 cards per pack, 216 cards total)
  • Shot at the complete 100-card base set covering Super Heroes, Arch-Enemies, Super-Villains, Teams, and Animated TV Series cards
  • 9 Gold Foil chase cards — Cable, Cyclops, Juggernaut, Magneto, Professor X, Rogue, The Sentinels, Storm, Wolverine
  • Hologram chase cards — Cable, Magneto, Storm (standard) plus the rare Wolverine 3-D Hologram in green and red variants

A standout 1990s Marvel set with strong long-term collector demand. The hologram chase cards in particular — especially the Wolverine 3-D variants — remain highly sought after.
